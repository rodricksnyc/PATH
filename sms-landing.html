Summary

Eventually, the test cases will be categorized as in the example below (Adult Cigarette Smoker, Adult Non-Smoker, etc.). Currently, these are just placeholders and all test cases are the same.

Clicking a cell will list cases that fit that category. Selecting the cell circled below will list Adult Cigarette Smoker cases that have not yet started the survey.

3. Launch the survey

Click on the ID in the first column to select a case.

To launch the survey, select the “Go to Survey” button. At the end of the survey, it should redirect automatically to the SMS. If your session in the SMS has timed out, then you will be required to sign in before working more test cases.
